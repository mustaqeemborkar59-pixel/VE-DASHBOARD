# **App Name**: ForkliftFlow

## Core Features:

- Service Request Submission: Allow users to submit service requests for forklift maintenance, specifying the issue and forklift details.
- Technician Assignment: Enable administrators to assign service requests to available technicians.
- Job Card Generation: Automatically generate job cards with detailed information on the forklift, the reported problem, and assigned technician.
- Maintenance Log: Automatically update logs regarding jobs and repairs done on the forklifts.
- AI Diagnostic tool: Provide a tool that gives technicians assistance to resolve the problems.
- Parts Inventory: Create and track your parts available in the inventory to resolve a job.
- Reporting: Generate weekly and monthly reports on repairs done, parts consumed, etc. 

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#1E3A8A) to evoke a sense of trust and reliability.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5), almost white, to maintain a clean, professional look.
- Accent color: Electric blue (#7DD3FC) to highlight interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif font, suitable for both headlines and body text because of its neutral, objective look.
- Use clean, line-based icons to represent different forklift parts and maintenance actions.
- Employ a grid-based layout to ensure consistent spacing and alignment.
- Use subtle transitions and loading animations to provide feedback during actions.
